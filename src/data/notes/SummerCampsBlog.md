### School is out!

Summer seasons are the busiest at work. We run consecutive back-to-back camps for nearly every week of summer. Topics taught include Minecraft & Roblox modding/scripting (my personal favorite to teach), additional game development camps, 3D modeling basic & advanced (TinkerCAD vs Blender), and a whole bunch of other camps.

### Passion

I teach about 15-25 kids on a daily basis for a 5-day period and it never gets old.

I've always been an introverted individual but since starting this role as a Coding Instructor, I've been brought far out into the deep end, past my comfort zone.

Seeing the kids light up with joy and genuinely want to come in and learn something that they _actually_ want to learn is invaluable. You feel the energy in the room and it instantly fires you up! Honestly, it brings out a memory in me when I first started my career in CS & SWE. Showing up to class early, notebook and laptop out, and eyes with burning curiosity.

### More than halfway there

As I write this, it is currently July 15, 2026, and summer camps are more than halfway done. Our last one runs in the first week of August, and then back to school – even for me!

### What I've learned

If you are in a position to make a difference and bring out joy in people, do it. You have no idea just how much of an impact you're truly making in the world. My motto growing up is to always do things out of genuineness, honesty, and purpose.
